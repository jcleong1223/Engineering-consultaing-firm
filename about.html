About AlphaBuilt Consultant

We offers building design expertise for a wide range of construction project to maximise structural integrity and usability. Our guiding principle has always been to create high-quality designs that are secure, economical, and simple to construct.

About Us

AlphaBuit Consultant

Build the future with AlphaBuilt

AlphaBuilt Consultant was incorporated in the year 2010. It is a design and construction company based in Malaysia. The company was founded by five professionally qualified civil engineers who graduated in different majors such as structural engineer, environmental engineer, water resource engineer, etc. with more than 10 years of combined working experience in the field of engineering contracts and trading.
