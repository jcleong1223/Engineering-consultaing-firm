Services

AlphaBuilt Consultant is a consulting and construction company that focuses on the engineering consultation, architectural design, civil and structural design for housing and public areas as well as the project construction management.

Civil & Structural Design

Design building by using Building Information Modelling (BIM). Also, roads & streets design and planning.

Architectural Design & Modelling

Housing design & modelling for residential area, and commercial building. Public buildings design & modelling such as hospitals and shopping malls.

Soil and Geotechnical Analysis

Evaluating soil conditions and subsurface. Also, investigating the risks or geological hazards.

Water Resource & Environmental Consulting

Water management system, water distribution system design and development. Flood routing system design and development, flood risk management.

AlphaBuit Consultant

Build the future with AlphaBuilt

AlphaBuilt Consultant was incorporated in the year 2010. It is a design and construction company based in Malaysia. The company was founded by five professionally qualified civil engineers who graduated in different majors such as structural engineer, environmental engineer, water resource engineer, etc. with more than 10 years of combined working experience in the field of engineering contracts and trading.
